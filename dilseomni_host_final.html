About the Host

Starting something new is never easy —
but always worth it.

Saurabh founded DAiOM with one simple idea: to redefine how data can drive meaningful omnichannel growth for businesses. Before building DAiOM, he worked across Lenskart, American Express, and the Tata Group — gaining deep experience in analytics, marketing, technology, and business growth.

Today, Saurabh is widely recognised as India's top omnichannel growth consultant — partnering with brands across retail, D2C, pharma, SaaS, and fintech to measure, optimise, and scale across online and offline channels.

Beyond consulting, he has guided 1,000+ professionals and students at ISB, IIMs, IITs and other leading institutions — and has been recognised among India's Top 100 AI Leaders and as a Top 1% Career Coach.

Why the podcast
Real growth comes from
clarity, curiosity,
and continuous learning.

— Saurabh Agrawal, Founder DAiOM


At DAiOM, as Saurabh worked with brands on real omnichannel challenges, a strong passion for sharing learnings emerged. Blogs, guides, and content followed — making complex growth topics simple and actionable.

To take this mission further, the idea of a podcast was born. DilSe Omni Talks was launched to bring expert conversations, industry insights, and practical frameworks to a wider audience — and has grown into one of India's most respected voices in the omnichannel growth space.

01
The problem with omnichannel content
Most growth content is theoretical. Saurabh wanted real conversations with real practitioners — people who had actually built and scaled omnichannel businesses.
02
Learning in public
The podcast became a way to document learnings from DAiOM's client work — distilling complex frameworks into accessible, actionable insights for founders and marketers.
03
Building a community of doers
DilSe Omni grew into a platform connecting India's brightest omnichannel minds — guests from CleverTap, Duroflex, Narayana Healthcare, Appbrew, and beyond.
04
From podcast to India's top omnichannel show
With time, DilSe Omni Talks strengthened Saurabh's position as India's leading omnichannel growth voice — now one of India's top podcasts in the omnichannel growth category.
