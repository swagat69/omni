Common questions

Quick answers.

How often does the newsletter go out? +
Once a month. We won't flood your inbox — just one well-curated email with episode recaps, frameworks, and what's happening in the omnichannel world.
I want to be a guest — should I fill in the contact form? +
Please use the dedicated Be a Guest application form instead — it's designed to capture the right information to review your application properly. You'll find it at the Be a Guest page.
How long does it take to get a response? +
We aim to respond to all messages within 3–5 business days. For guest applications, it's 5–7 business days. If you haven't heard back after that, feel free to follow up.
Do you work with sponsors or brand partners? +
Yes, selectively. We partner with brands that are genuinely relevant to the omnichannel community — not paid placement for unrelated products. If you'd like to explore a partnership, please reach out via the contact form with your proposal.
Can I republish or share episode content? +
Yes — you're welcome to share clips, quotes, and show notes with appropriate credit to Dilse Omni Talks and a link back to the episode page. For anything beyond standard sharing, please reach out first.
Where is Dilse Omni Talks based? +
Dilse Omni Talks is produced by DAiOM, based in India. Episodes are recorded in our studio or remotely via video call — guests join from across India and beyond.
