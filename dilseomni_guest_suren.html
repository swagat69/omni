Topics Covered

What we discussed.

01
The offline conversion funnel — how to think about footfall the same way you think about web traffic, and what to measure at each step.
02
What Tango Eye does — Google Analytics for physical stores, using existing CCTV cameras to track zones, dwell time, and conversion.
03
Operational tech stack — staff attendance, process checklists, footfall demographics — the basics most brands still haven't systemized.
04
Customer-facing tech — virtual try-on, endless aisle, appointment booking, self-checkout — what's worth investing in and why.
05
The biggest mistake brands make — spending months evaluating tech, then compiling it into a presentation that nobody acts on.
06
Where AI is taking store tech — from data collection to AI-assisted decisions on staffing, product placement, and store expansion.
