Who should apply

We want practitioners,
not theorists.

Dilse Omni is not a panel discussion. It's a real conversation. We want people who have been in the trenches — who have made decisions, seen the results, and have something honest to share.

Founders & CEOs
Who have built or scaled omnichannel retail, D2C, or commerce brands — and can share the real decisions, not just the highlight reel.
CMOs & Growth Leaders
With battle-tested frameworks in martech, retention, loyalty, or CX — people who can talk about what works and what doesn't from direct experience.
Product & Tech Builders
Creating tools, platforms, or infrastructure that's changing how modern retail and commerce works — from analytics to AI to in-store tech.
Investors & Operators
Who have a unique perspective on where omnichannel commerce is heading, backed by data or a portfolio of brands they've worked with closely.

The process

Simple.
Straightforward.

01
Submit your application
Fill in the form above with your background, the topic you'd cover, and a brief description of your story. Takes 3 minutes.
02
Team review (5–7 business days)
Saurabh's team reviews every application personally. We'll reach out by email if your profile is a fit for an upcoming episode.
03

A 20-minute call with Saurabh to align on topics, talking points, and what you want the audience to take away. No scripts — just direction.

Record the episode
A relaxed, 45–60 minute conversation — recorded in our studio or via video call. Saurabh leads, you share. It's always candid, never scripted.
05
Edit, publish & distribute
We handle post-production, show notes, SEO, and distribution across YouTube, Spotify, and Apple Podcasts. You get the link to share.
